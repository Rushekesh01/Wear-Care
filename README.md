1.	Project Title: 
Wear & Care:A Clothing Donation Initiative

3.	Abstract:
Wear & Care is a web-based application designed to promote textile sustainability and support underprivileged communities by bridging the gap between clothing donors and those in need. Huge volumes of wearable clothing are discarded annually in landfills, while many individuals lack access to basic apparel. This system provides a centralized digital platform where individuals, retail brands, and organizations can list surplus or pre-loved garments. The platform features secure user authentication, unique email validation, and encrypted data handling to ensure a safe environment for all participants. An integrated admin module allows for the moderation of listings and monitoring of donation cycles. By streamlining the logistics of clothing redistribution, Wear & Care fosters a culture of circular fashion, reduces environmental waste, and strengthens community welfare through technological intervention.

4.	Domain:
Web and Application Development

5.	Application: 
This project connects clothing donors (individuals, garment manufacturers, retail stores) with charitable organizations, shelters, and needy individuals.
The system allows users to:
•	Register and log in through a secure portal.
•	List available clothing specifying type (winter, formal, etc.), size, and condition.
•	Browse and claim clothing items based on specific needs.
•	Track donation history and impact through a personalized dashboard.
It serves as a digital warehouse that optimizes the lifecycle of garments and ensures they reach the right hands efficiently.

6.	Objectives:
•	To develop an online platform for clothing donation.
•	To connect clothing donors with NGOs and needy people.
•	To allow donors to upload details of clothes such as type, size, and condition.
•	To implement a secure user registration and login system.
•	To enable receivers to view and request available clothes.
•	To promote reuse of clothes and reduce clothing waste.
•	To support social welfare and community help.
